SAP HANA Optimization Using Statistical Estimation (HOUSE)

Completed comprehensive proof-of-concepts in C++ to corroborate the research claims. The paper for this research is aimed for submission in SIGMOD 2015 (under corporate track).

SpeakUp

Whitepaper

A sign-language to speech conversion system, SpeakUp is a cost-effective glove-based system to convert the static subset of a sign language to voice. To record the different values of resistance during different hand gestures, special cost effective gloves were fabricated with self-developed uni-directional flexion (bend) sensors. 'Statistical Template Matching' technique was employed for processing and recognizing various hand gestures.

Qubo - Quadruped Robot

QUBO is a 4-legged voice controlled robot for entertainment and basic security purposes. Built on the open-source Arduino platform and equipped with on-board sensors such as a micro-camera, microphone and touch sensor, QUBO can interact with humans through voice & images. It also exhibit a large variety of behavioral patterns and has various features like the ability to recognize its master's voice, marker detection, collision intelligence, etc.

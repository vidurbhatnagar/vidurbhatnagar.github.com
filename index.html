Project Portfolio

SAP Sports Analytics for Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)

As a fellow in the Managing Director's office at SAP Labs India, I was implementing predictive algorithms on SAP HANA, to analyze 6 years of historical data from different leagues (IPL, BigBash, etc.), to help the cricket franchisee (KKR) in analyzing strengths and weaknesses of different teams and individual players.

The entire portoflio consisted of 3 solutions viz. SAP Auction Analytics - which helped KKR choose one of the best teams for the season, SAP Game Analytics - to define on-field strategy for every game, and SAP Game Visualizations -a fan-engagement solution.

SAP HANA Optimization Using Statistical Estimation (HOUSE)

Patent Applications: US 14/098,706; US 14/314,032


Research project to optimize dictionary compression in SAP HANA (SAP's flagship in-memory platform) through ingenious memory reorganization based on the statistics of unique strings in the dictionary. Completed comprehensive proof-of-concepts in C++ to corroborate the research claims. The paper for this research is aimed for submission in SIGMOD 2015 (under corporate track).

SpeakUp

Whitepaper


A sign-language to speech conversion system, SpeakUp is a cost-effective glove-based system to convert the static subset of a sign language to voice. To record the different hand gestures, special cost effective gloves were fabricated with self-developed uni-directional flex (bend) sensors. 'Statistical Template Matching' technique was employed for processing and then recognizing these gestures.

Qubo - Quadruped Robot

QUBO is a 4-legged voice controlled robot for entertainment and basic security purposes. Built on the open-source Arduino platform and equipped with on-board sensors such as a micro-camera, microphone and touch sensor, QUBO can interact with humans through voice & images. It also exhibit a large variety of behavioral patterns and has various features like the ability to recognize its master's voice, marker detection, collision intelligence, etc.

This helped the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) team in analyzing strengths and weaknesses of different teams and individual players during the auction process, and while selecting the playing eleven. KKR won back to back tournaments, and publicly credited our technology for their success.

Within 3 months of joining as an FTE, I was selected as the Employee of the Year in 2013, across 4500+ SAP Labs India employees.
